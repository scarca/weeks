Weeks

Life is finite. So finite, in fact, that all the weeks in your life can be displayed in just a few rows. Each of these rows represents one year of your life, and a red dot will show you how far along you are. There are 90 rows for 90 years by default, but the average life expectancy changes based on sex -- 84.3 years for males, and 86.6 years for females 1. Make the most of your time.
